Creating Global Opportunities with a Unified Passion

Christina Wang was born in Northern California and has been involved in the creative industry since she was born. She was involved in the dance world ever since she was 3 and that creative industry inspired her to get involved in the entertainment industry. She decided to move to Southern California to pursue her dreams of working in Hollywood during her college years. She has worked in all sorts of entertainment fields such as: music videos, feature films, short films, commercials, youtube, film festivals, talent competitions, and performances. Christina has knowledge in all stages of production from pre to post. She has a variety of skills she's obtained through working in the different fields such as: photography, stage management, talent management, marketing, and public relations. Christina studied abroad all over the world to gain knowledge of the different entertainment industries and pop culture so she could bring the knowledge back to America, as well as gaining networking partners for future collaborations. Christina's ultimate goal is to bring more diversity into Hollywood so there can be more diverse representation in mainstream media. Christina is hoping to become the bridge that can bring more Asian talent represntation into Hollywood, as well as bringing famous talented Hollywood actors into working in sets in other parts of the world.

Her hobbies include dancing, photography, video production, badminton, binge watching shows on Netflix, cooking, traveling, and discovering hidden cafes and restaurants.
